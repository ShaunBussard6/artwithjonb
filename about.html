About Jon

Jon has been both a fan and creator of art since he was a child, and excelled as a photographer for the majoirty of his professional career. Recently, he has been able to become a full time painter, doing 6X6 pet portraits for Chewy.com. Being an artist full time has been a dream come true for Jon, and he has just recently passed 500 paintings in the past year, completing more paintings in one year, than he had in his entire life up to that point. Jon's full time focus on his craft has given him the ability to hone in on his craft, and you can see it in the detail and expert lighting of his paintings.

Jon was born and raised in Columbus Ohio, before moving to Phoenix for Photography, and later Salt Lake City, where he had his son, Shaun. Jon and Shaun now reside in Fort Lauderdale Florida. If Jon is not painting, he is either watching sports with Shaun, or going for bike rides along the beach. Jons expertise with art is in portraits, but he also excels at archetecture and landscape pieces, he is also open to doing custom art pieces. If you are interested in getting some art by Jon, feel free to fill out the contact form on the contact page of this website, or contact him via the phone or email address information available below. Thank you for checking out Jons work!
